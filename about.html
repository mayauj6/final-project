Appearances are deceiving.

Domestic Violence happens behind closed doors. While on the surface, a domestic situation may appear safe, what lies beneath can be dangerous, harmful, and even life threatening.

Why Hover?

You may have been confused when you discovered the hidden hover elements on our page. We wanted to create a site that visually simulates the harsh reality of domestic violence. The bright colors and fake cover "Family Wellness" page represents the way domestic violence is a hidden layer below a seemingly picture perfect situation

Why EMMber?

The name for our site sprung from an acronym of our first names: Ella, Maya, and Manlin, or the EMM in EMMber. Ember, a small piece of glowing coal in fire, to us represents the hope and potential domestic violence victims have and how even as their fire is dying, there is potential to ignite a fire and invoke change to escape their situation.

You Can't Ignore the Layers

Domestic violence can be so easy for people to ignore. There are often no witnesses, and getting involed seems like it hurt more than it would help. However, much like our website, it should be impossible to ignore the hidden layers. Yet, by publicly speaking out against domestic violence, together we can challenge attitudes towards violence in the home and show that domestic violence is a crime and not merely unacceptable.
