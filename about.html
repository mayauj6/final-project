Appearances are deceiving.

Domestic Violence happens behind closed doors. While on the surface, a domestic situation may appear safe, what lies beneath can be dangerous, harmful, and even life threatening.

Why Hover?

We wanted to create a site that visually simulates the harsh reality of domestic violence. The bright colors and fake cover "Family Wellness" page represents the way doemstic violence are a hidden layer below a seemingly picture perfect situation

Why EMMber?

The name for our site sprung from an acronym of our first names: Ella, Maya, and Manlin, or the EMM in EMMber. Ember, a small piece of glowing coal in fire, to us represents the hope and potential domestic violence victims have and how even as their fire is dying, there is potential to ignite a fire and invoke change to escape their situation.
